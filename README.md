# django-crud-app-beleaf

#Background 
* Beleaf is a plant tracking app with the aim of helping users keep    their plants alive - something I am yet to succeed at! 
* Users can sign up to the app and then create a list of their plants, record the care they've given their plants and also assign plants to a category. 
* Users can create/edit/update plants and categories. 
* All plant information and category information is specific to the user and cannot be seen by other users. 
* The logo for the app is a plant emoji 🪴
* Technologies used: Python and Django 

* Next Steps: 
    - A community aspect would definitely be a positive way to develop the app, for example users being able to view other user's plants, or a thread giving advice on looking after plants. 
    - Images: it would be interesting to explore APIs and the possibility for images to automatically generate when a user selects a category or types in a commonly known plant. 
    - Reminder notifications: if users included the required amount of care for their plants then emails / prompts could be generated to remind them if they miss giving their plant the required care.
